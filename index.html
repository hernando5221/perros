    The physical characteristics of this dog are unmistakable. The long, pointed head and the small eyes seen in it are two unique features of the Bull Terrier. The end of the snout also stands out, where you can see a small black nose. The small triangular ears of this breed are always raised, allowing the dog to always be aware of everything that happens around him.

    It is one of the dogs that shows the most loyalty to its owner and his family. Although it has a rough appearance, it is usually a very gentle and sweet dog. It has a quite remarkable intelligence, which should be used to educate it since it is a puppy.

    The robust appearance of the Rottweiler is one of the hallmarks of this breed. It can be seen that the figure of the animal is longer than it is tall, in a set where the musculature is the main protagonist, a characteristic that can be easily verified in the legs, chest, trunk and belly.

    The corpulence of the Rottweiler makes it a very imposing dog and causes it to have a bad reputation in certain social sectors. It is said that it is a very aggressive dog even with the members of its family, however, these types of problems are determined by a bad education of the dog.

    The Chihuahua is characterized by being one of the smallest dog breeds that exist in the world. In addition to their small size, these dogs do not have consistent muscles, a factor that causes a sensation of fragility in the animal's body.

    The training of the dog from its puppy age is essential so that the Chihuahua does not become a rabid and protesting pet. His intelligence is a plus for education to be fast and effective, although he also knows how to use it to get what he wants. It is a very territorial dog. Despite its small size, the Chihuahua tends to show a defiant attitude towards other unfamiliar dogs, regardless of whether they triple in height.

    The Yorkshire Terrier is one of the best-known small dog breeds in the world. His tiny body stands out for the hardness of his belly and for how arched his ribs are shown. The legs, with small and short dimensions, show black nails on rounded feet. For its part, the tail is thin and short.

    Although her tiny body gives us a feeling of sweetness and tranquility, this is not always the case. The Yorkshire Terrier stands out for being a quite restless dog that only shows love to its own –especially to its owner-, while it rejects strangers with barking and bad humor.
